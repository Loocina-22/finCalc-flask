About Loan Payment

This calculator determines the fixed monthly payment for a loan based on the principal, annual interest rate, and loan term. It’s useful for planning payments for mortgages, car loans, or personal loans.

Formula

\[ M = P \cdot \frac{r (1 + r)^n}{(1 + r)^n - 1} \]

Where:
\( M \): Monthly payment
\( P \): Principal
\( r \): Monthly interest rate (annual rate ÷ 12)
\( n \): Number of months (years × 12)
