About Savings Goal

This planner calculates the monthly savings needed to reach a financial goal, accounting for compound interest. It’s perfect for planning for retirement, a vacation, or major purchases.

Formula

\[ S = FV \cdot \frac{r}{(1 + r)^n - 1} \]

Where:
\( S \): Monthly savings
\( FV \): Future value (goal)
\( r \): Monthly interest rate (annual rate ÷ 12)
\( n \): Number of months (years × 12)
